Elizabeth A. Tagoe
(Founder & Proprietress)

The founder and head of Global Training Institute completed St. Anne's Vocational Training Institute at Odumase Krobo in the Eastern Region of Ghana in 1982 and worked with FLORICA Catering and Dressmaking Services for four years. She then started her own business namely Global Care-Takers Centre in 1986, which is now Global Training Institute.

Our Main Objective

GTI is a Vocational Training Institution with the main objective of training young ladies and women from the following background;

  • Student and Graduates from Junior and Senior High Schools in Ghana.
  • Those who cannot afford any formal education and have no profession or vocation that can earn them a living.
  • Other women like workers and housewives who want a second profession or vocation to increase their sources of income.

Our Training Objectives

Our training objectives are to practically train women to acquire vocational and employable skills, to instill basic Godly disciplines, virtues and values into their lives and to educate and motivate them to become productive and responsible in life.
